Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my bike?

+

For regular riders, we recommend a basic tune-up every 3-4 months and a standard service annually. If you ride frequently or in challenging conditions, more frequent servicing may be necessary. E-bikes typically require service every 500-1000 miles.

How long does a service typically take?

+

A basic tune-up usually takes 1-2 hours, while a standard service takes 24-48 hours. Premium services and custom builds may take 3-5 days depending on the complexity and parts availability. We'll provide a specific timeframe when you drop off your bike.

Do you provide warranty on your services?

+

Yes, all our services come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If any issues arise related to our service within this period, we'll fix them at no additional cost. Parts replaced during service carry the manufacturer's warranty.

Can I bring my own parts for installation?

+

Yes, we can install customer-supplied parts. However, please note that we cannot provide warranty on these parts. We recommend using our quality parts for optimal performance and warranty coverage.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer pickup and delivery within a 10-mile radius of our shop for a small additional fee. This service is complimentary for premium service packages. Please inquire about availability when booking your service.
